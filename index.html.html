How to Make Slowed + Reverb Songs

Making a slowed and reverb song is simple with this tool:

Step 1: Click the upload area or drag and drop your MP3, WAV, FLAC, or OGG file.

Step 2: Select the Slowed preset (or choose Nightcore, Daycore, Lo-Fi, or Sped Up).

Step 3: Fine-tune speed, pitch, reverb mix, reverb size, and bass boost with the sliders.

Step 4: Click Process Audio and preview the result with the built-in player.

Step 5: Download your remix as a high-quality WAV file, free.
